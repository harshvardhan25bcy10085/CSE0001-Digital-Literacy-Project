## Project Overview

This project is part of the Digital Literacy course and focuses on building essential digital skills required for students. The objective is to understand the safe and effective use of technology, create a professional digital presence, and explore online tools for learning and collaboration.

The project consists of five tasks, each covering a different aspect of digital literacy, including design, portfolio development, coding practice, communication, and cybercrime awareness.

Task Summary

Task 1 – Infographic

Designed a digital literacy awareness infographic using Canva. The infographic explains the meaning of digital literacy and includes important topics such as useful digital tools for students, safe internet practices, maintaining a professional online presence, and email etiquette. The focus was on making the design visually clear, well-structured, and easy to understand for students.

Task 2 – Digital Portfolio

Created and updated profiles on LinkedIn, GitHub, and Stack Overflow to build a strong professional digital presence. Added basic academic details, skills, and profile information. GitHub was used to create a profile README, while LinkedIn was updated with education details. These platforms will help in networking, showcasing work, and preparing for internships and future opportunities.

Task 3 – Platforms

Completed a beginner-level coding challenge (“Solve Me First”) on HackerRank to practice basic programming concepts such as input, output, and logic building. Also created a Google Form quiz on digital literacy with multiple-choice and short-answer questions. This task helped in improving coding skills as well as understanding how to create and manage online quizzes.

Task 4 – Email Etiquette

Wrote two professional emails based on real-life scenarios and followed proper email etiquette, including clear subject lines, formal tone, and structured content. Additionally, created a checklist of do’s and don’ts for responsible digital communication. This task helped in understanding how to communicate effectively and professionally in academic and professional settings.

Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness

Developed a case study on phishing, explaining how such attacks occur, who is targeted, and their consequences. Also created a “Stay Safe Online” checklist with practical prevention tips, including awareness about financial and UPI safety. This task improved understanding of cyber threats and how to stay safe online.
