Stay Safe Online — Checklist for College Students (India)

General Safety Tips:

1) Use strong, unique passwords for each account (combine letters, numbers, symbols)
2) Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on email, social media, and banking apps
3) Always verify links before clicking — check spelling and domain names carefully
4) Avoid using public Wi-Fi for logging into sensitive accounts (banking, email, etc.)
5) Keep your devices updated with the latest software and antivirus protection
6) Think before posting — avoid sharing personal details (college ID, address, phone number)
7) Download apps only from official sources like Google Play Store or Apple App Store
8) Be cautious of "urgent” messages asking for information or money — they are often scams

---

UPI & Financial Safety Tips (Important)

1) Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone — even if they claim to be from a bank
2) Always check whether you are “paying” or “receiving” — UPI collect requests can be fake
3) Do not scan unknown QR codes sent via WhatsApp or social media
4) Set a daily transaction limit and enable SMS/email alerts for every transaction

---

If Something Goes Wrong:

Call Helpline: 1930 immediately for financial fraud
Report online at National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in/Webform/Accept.aspx

---

Following these simple steps can greatly reduce your risk and help you stay safe in the digital world.
