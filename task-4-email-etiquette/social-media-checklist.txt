A) Do’s for Responsible Social Media Use:
1. Think before you post, ensure your content is respectful and appropriate
2. Protect your privacy, adjust settings and limit personal information shared
3. Verify information before sharing, avoid spreading fake news
4. Be respectful in comments and messages, maintain digital etiquette
5. Use social media for learning and networking, follow useful pages/accounts


B) Don’ts for Responsible Social Media Use
1. Don’t share sensitive personal details (address, passwords, phone number)
2. Don’t engage in cyberbullying or online arguments
3. Don’t believe or forward unverified information blindly
4. Don’t post inappropriate or offensive content
5. Don’t spend excessive time, avoid social media addiction
